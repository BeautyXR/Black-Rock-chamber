Who We Are

A Firm Built on Precision,
Integrity & Purpose

Jakpa, Edoge & Co., professionally known as Black Rock Chambers, is a full-service Nigerian law firm headquartered in Warri, Delta State, with a branch office in the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja.

For over two decades, we have delivered strategic, client-focused legal solutions grounded in precision, integrity, and commercial awareness. From our roots as a litigation-focused practice, we have grown into a respected legal institution serving individuals, corporations, communities, and public bodies across Nigeria — particularly within the Niger Delta region.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional legal services with integrity, dedication, and expertise that genuinely serves our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading law firm in the Niger Delta region, known for excellence, integrity, and client-centered service.

Our Values

Integrity, diligence, and excellence guide every instruction we receive and every matter we handle.

Our Reach

Headquartered in Warri with a branch in FCT Abuja, serving clients across Nigeria and the Niger Delta.

What We Do

Our Practice Areas

Our multidisciplinary team of legal practitioners brings decades of combined expertise across Nigeria's most complex and commercially significant areas of law — from the oil-rich Niger Delta to the corridors of corporate Nigeria.

01

Maritime & Oil and Gas Law

Specialist legal counsel for operators, contractors, and communities across Nigeria's oil-rich Niger Delta. From regulatory compliance to dispute resolution — we know this terrain.

02

Corporate & Commercial Law

Strategic legal solutions for businesses at every stage — from formation and structuring to corporate governance, mergers, acquisitions, and commercial contracts.

03

Civil & Commercial Litigation

Vigorous advocacy in court and beyond. We approach litigation as a powerful strategic tool to enforce rights, recover value, and bring disputes to decisive resolution.

04

Property & Real Estate Law

We help clients navigate Nigeria's complex real estate landscape — from title verification and conveyancing to dispute resolution and development agreements.

05

Community Engagement & Local Content

Strengthening company-community relations with legal clarity across Nigeria's resource-rich regions. We mediate, advise, and draft frameworks that protect all parties.

06

Legal Documentation & Contract Drafting

Agreements that stand scrutiny. We draft, review, and negotiate contracts that protect our clients' interests with precision — from commercial leases to multi-party joint ventures.

Emmanuel Jakpa, Esq.
Legal Luminary & Strategic Adviser

At the forefront of Black Rock Chambers is Emmanuel Jakpa, Esq. — an accomplished legal practitioner, strategic thinker, and seasoned investor whose career spans more than two decades of dedicated service at the Bar.

A graduate of the University of Benin and an alumnus of the Nigerian Law School, Bwari, Barrister Jakpa combines deep legal expertise with commercial acuity and governance insight. He has built a reputation for incisive legal reasoning, astute strategic counsel, and effective risk management across complex regulatory and corporate landscapes.
